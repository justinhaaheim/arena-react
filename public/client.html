The Coaching Arena

Qualities of Being

Arena Questions

  1. There are some qualities I am willing to demonstrate in this interaction so we can both make sure we have an extraordinary result out of our session together: I am willing to be: (I am willing to be None)
  2. During the session am I willing to set aside the opinions, beliefs, and thoughts that may be getting in the way of seeing something clearly? (Choose 'Yes' or 'No')
  3. Am I willing to be open-minded and fully engaged in order to receive maximum benefit from this time together? (Choose 'Yes' or 'No')
  4. The result of coaching in this model is always action. Am I willing to see the most authentic action to take? If I am the person being coached, am I willing to take it? (Choose 'Yes' or 'No')
